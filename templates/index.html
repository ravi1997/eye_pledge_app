Welcome to Eye Donation

Eye donation is a noble deed that helps restore sight to the blind and contributes to medical research. By pledging your eyes, you give the gift of vision to those in need.

Why Donate Your Eyes?

  • Restore Sight: Corneal transplants can help blind people see again.
  • Medical Research: Eye tissue helps advance vision science and treatment options.
  • No Age Barrier: Eyes can be used regardless of age or corrective lens use.
  • Revocable Decision: You can revoke your pledge at any time.
Important Information

Eye donation can be done only after death. Your family will be contacted at the time of death if you have pledged your eyes.

How to Pledge?

  1. Fill out the Eye Donation Pledge Form with your details
  2. Provide information about your family and witnesses
  3. Declare your consent and understanding
  4. You will receive a unique reference number for your pledge
