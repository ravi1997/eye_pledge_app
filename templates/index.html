Impact

Why donate your eyes?

Restore sight

Corneal transplants allow people with corneal blindness to regain vision and independence.

Advance research

Eye tissue fuels breakthroughs in vision science and better treatment options.

No age barrier

Pledges are accepted at any age and regardless of corrective lenses.

Fully revocable

Change your mind at any time—your pledge respects your latest decision.

Important to know

Eye donation takes place only after death. Your family will be contacted when needed if you have pledged your eyes.
